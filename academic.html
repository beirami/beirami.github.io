Academic Engagements

Ahmad Beirami is an active researcher in AI, with contributions spanning foundational research to practical applications. His work has received multiple best paper awards, and he has delivered keynote talks at major venues. He has also organized workshops and tutorials, and served in leadership roles at leading conferences, including ACL and NeurIPS.
