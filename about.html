About

Ahmad Beirami is a technologist passionate about building innovative, efficient, and scalable products. With 15+ years of building AI systems and 5+ years of hands-on experience building large language model (LLM) products at Google and Meta, he bridges deep technical expertise with product impact at scale. His most recent work on LLM alignment was launched in Google Gemini models and earned an Outstanding Paper Award at ICLR 2025.
Selected Product Achievements
  • Google DeepMind: Created and launched controlled decoding for alignment of Google Gemini models that pushed up the Pareto frontier of model capabilities.
  • Google Research: Created and launched speculative decoding solutions that made inference 2x faster from Gemini and Gemma models.
  • Meta AI: Created and portotyped CAIRaoke, the first digital assistant with tool-use capabilities at Meta.
  • Electronic Arts: Launched the first RL solutions for pre-launch playtesting in EA games to find bugs and balance resources.
