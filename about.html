About

Ahmad Beirami is a technologist passionate about building innovative, efficient, and scalable products. With over 15 years of experience in AI, more than 5 years of hands-on development of large language model (LLM) products at Google and Meta, and building an award-winning AI agent framework at Electronic Arts, he bridges deep technical expertise with real-world product impact. His recent work on LLM alignment advanced the Pareto frontier of Gemini model capabilities and earned an Outstanding Paper Award at ICLR 2025.

Selected Product Achievements

Ahmad Beirami has led dozens of product launches that span RL in games, integrity of video games, classifier fairness/robustness, and language model alignment/efficiency.

  • Google DeepMind: Created and launched controlled decoding and InfAlign for alignment of Google Gemini models that pushed up the Pareto frontier of model capabilities and inference-time compute scaling.
  • Google Research: Created and launched speculative decoding solutions that made inference 2x faster from Gemini and Gemma models.
  • Meta AI: Created and portotyped CAIRaoke, the first digital assistant agent with tool-use capabilities at Meta.
  • Electronic Arts: Launched the first RL agents for pre-launch playtesting of EA games to find bugs and balance resources.
